Chicken Orizo Soup

A fresh and lemony recipe, which complements the spring time perfectly.

Ingredients:

    1 Onion 1/2 Celery 2 cloves of garlic 2 carrots 1 TS of toasted coriander seeds chicken broth 1 cup of orzo pasta cilantro dill/ls> lemon 2 bay leaves

How to make it

Step one

Saute the onions until they look seethrough. Add the other vegetables.

Step two

Add the chicken broth and the chicken and cook it unil the chicken can be pulled. Pull the chicken meat.

Step three

Toast you coriander seeds, crush themand add them to the stock along with the lemon zest.

Step four

Add the orizo and let it cook until al dente.

Step five

Serve the soup with freshly chopoped herbs and a splash of lemon juice.
