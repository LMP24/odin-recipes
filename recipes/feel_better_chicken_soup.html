Feel better Chicken Soup

For decades, it was always chicken soup that left us feeling better when we had a cold or were feeling under the weather. Every family has their own recipe that they pass on to each new genreration. Here you will find my chicken soup recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • veggies like celery or carrots
  • chicken broth
  • 2 pices ofchicken breast
  • parsley
  • lemon

How to make it

Step one

Saute the onions until they look seethrough. Add the other veggies and stir fry themfor a few minutes until they develope their flavor.

Step two

Add the chicken broth and the chicken. Bring it to simmer and let it cook for 20 minutes, until the chicken is cooked.

Step three

Final, serve the chicken with freshherbs and a splsh of lemon.
