Birria Tacos

A traditional Mexican tortilla recipe made with either lamb or beef stew. The stew can be cooked in the oven, in a pressure cooker or even a slow cooker.

Ingredients:

    1 Onion 2 cloves of garlic Mexican dried chilis. Guajillo chiles or ancho chilies, or Pasilla chilies, chipotles chicken/beef broth beef 1 can of diced tomatoes spices: oregano, black pepper, cumin, coriander, bay leaves, chili powder, cayenne pepper and cinnamon lemon honey

How to make it

Step one

Saute the roughly chopped onions and garlic until they look seethrough.

Step two

Add the spices, let it cook for 1-2 minutes, then add the canned tomatoes.

Step three

Season the meat, cut it into cubes. Add it to the cookingpot with the stock.

Step four

Remove the stems from the dried chillies and slightly toast them until they rlease their aromatic oil. Th√©n add them tothe pot.

Step five

Let it all cook for 3 hours. The meat should always be coverd with soup. If that is not the case add some water.

Step six

Remove the chilies, blend them with a little bit of the stock, then add it again to the soup.

Step seven

Before serving shred the meat. Serve the meat with cilantro, finely chopped onions, beans and cheese in a soft taco.
